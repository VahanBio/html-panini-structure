WHO WE ARE

Our cattle are raised with a holistic approach on open regenerative pastures in a low stress environment. These cows never receive any antibiotics or growth hormones. If for some reason they do need antibiotics, they are immediately removed from our beef program

Through years of advancing our herd genetics, we have found that our angus based herd produces an easy feshing animal that marbles well and is enjoyable for everyone
